The Debtors

The Problem

When a company is on the brink of administration, directors often wait too long to act. 

By then, the bank or creditors have already moved, appointing their own administrator. 

Directors lose control, their business is broken up, and they risk wrongful trading claims, misfeasance actions, or personal guarantees being enforced.

Without early intervention, directors are left exposed and powerless.

The Solution

Emqore steps in pre-appointment to protect directors and preserve value.

We install a rescue-minded administrator from our coalition, giving directors influence over the process before it’s too late.

We provide legal firepower to shield directors personally while structuring survival strategies such as pre-pack sales, CVAs, or restructuring plans.

We support directors, conserve the business, and stop hostile creditors from dictating the outcome.

The Result

With Emqore, directors and companies help shape their own insolvency, stay onside with creditors as well as administrators, and take back control of their cash-flows.
